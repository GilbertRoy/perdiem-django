FAQ

  • Q: How is this different than crowdfunding platforms like Kickstarter or Indiegogo?

    • Traditional crowdfunding platforms only allow you to "donate" or "pre-order" music through perks by artists. With PerDiem you are actually investing in a project and can make a monetary return if the project is successful. This creates an incentive for the investors to promote the music long term since the success of their investment is tied to the success of the artist.

  • Q: So do I own equity in the artists?

    • No, you don't own any equity in the artist or their music. The artist sets a percentage they want to give back to the investors based on revenue for that project. Check out the "How It Works" page to get a better understanding of how this works.

  • Q: Can I re-invest my money earned into another artist?

    • Yes. We encourage you to re-invest and we will cover the cost if you decide to re-invest so that more artists can benefit from the platform.

  • Q: Is there a cap on the number of shares I can purchase?

    • You can purchase up to 500 shares in an artist.

  • Q: What happens if the artist signs to a record label?

    • It's totally up to the artist. Every label is different in the deal they negotiate with artists. Since the music is already released, it's unlikely that a label would want to pull it and collect revenue from a past project. If they do, you can simply give your remaining shares to the label or work out a deal with your investors where everyone wins.

  • Q: What happens if an artist leaves the platform?

    • The system is designed to protect both the artists and the fans. If an artist decides to leave the platform we highly encourage them to do it in a way that doesn't upset or disappoint their investors. These are the people that believed in you before anyone else and will be some of your most loyal fans.

  • Q: Is this legal?

    • Yes. It is 100% legal. Since the band retains full ownership of their music, and makes the choice to "give back" to their fans, there is no need to have contracts between the artists and the fans. You are simply purchasing a record (or multiple records) from the artist and the artist is rewarding you for doing so. Be sure to read over the Terms & Conditions to learn even more about how this works.

  • Q: How does PerDiem make money?

    • 100% of the money from shares purchased goes to the artists, and 100% of revenue is split between the artist and fans. The only additional cost for the fans is the credit card processing fee + $1 to make the transaction (this $1 covers costs of keeping the platform running).

      For example: You invest $10 in an artist. It costs $1.41 to process that transaction. $10 goes to the artist, $1 goes to Perdiem, $.41 goes to the credit card company.

  • Q: What if the artist doesn't reach their goal?

    • If an artist doesn't reach their goal, they would not be able produce the album. Any money invested would be refunded to the fans who invested in them.

  • Q: Who owns the rights to the artists music?

    • The artist owns full rights to their music.

  • Q: How much time does an artist have to reach their goal?

    • We recommend setting a 30 day campaign. This increases your chances of getting funded.

  • Q: Once I invest, how do I get money?

    • If you decide to sell your shares, we purchase them back from you at their current value. Then we give these shares back to the artist.

      You then have the option to re-invest your earnings, or be sent a check for the value of your shares.

  • Q: Once an artist gets funded, how do they get money?

    • We send the artist a check for the full amount they raised.
